Interest in IT

What is your interest in IT? I’m interested in how IT is constantly evolving. Although the progress of IT back in the day may have been extremely slow nowadays things are upgrading faster than ever and I want to work in an environment that reflects just that. I’d constantly be learning new things and I’d never have a stale day at work. Alongside this I enjoy figuring out how things work which is why I’m very interested in the programming aspect of IT.

When did your interest in IT start? A long long time ago when I was a youngling.

Was there a particular event or person that sparked your interest? I wanted my own laptop since my brother had just started High School and got his own but no one wanted to give me one. Why did a primary school student need one anyway? Even though no one decided to get a laptop eventually my uncle found one of his old work laptops and let me keep it. It was then that my love for IT sparked. I mean like, I had always liked computer class in primary school but that old laptop was what really sold it for me. It had so many things wrong with it that I was constantly researching and diagnosing it.

Why did you choose to come to RMIT? My brother also went here for his Associate’s Degree and told me that it was the best place to go for anything IT related. I had also attended the open days and really liked what I saw. Alongside this, I was at RMIT last year for my High School classes so I was already a bit familiar with the layout. And you’ve got those cool rocking chairs on that one floor which has become my new favourite place to work at.

What do you expect to learn during your studies? Lots of different types of IT-related things. Almost like a bit of everything? Of course I’ll major in an area that interests me the most but the Bachelor of Information Technology is, in my opinion, extremely broad and has the first year covering all different aspects of IT. I like this approach though as it allows people to experience everything before deciding what they want to major in.

My Ideal Job

Why is this position appealing? This job advertisement is for an entry-level Developer position for someone who is experienced in either C++, Java or Javascript. The job is based in Japan and will require the successful applicant to either already be living in Japan or willing to move there under a working visa. Knowledge of the Japanese language is not required but will be highly preferred. This position is appealing to me because it’s literally exactly what I’ve been dreaming of. A coding position, located in Japan with the promise of a working visa. And they’re open to hiring recent graduates! Alongside this, they say that knowledge of the Japanese language is not required but I would love to continue studying my Japanese and what better place to learn than Japan itself.

What are the required skills, qualifications and experience for this position? Business level English, preferably some knowledge of the Japanese language and experience in either C++ on Linux, Java, Javascript or C# (back end).

What skills, qualifications and experience do you currently have? Business level English, knowledge of the Japanese language and very very little experience in Java.

What is your plan in obtaining the required skills, qualifications and experience required for this position? Practice my English :”) Practice and keep up to date with my Japanese studies and find out which programming language interests me the most out of the ones required for the job. Also gain some knowledge with the Linux OS in preparation for the future. Major in programming/developing specifically to make me look more appealing as a candidate.
